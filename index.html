Decentralized Finance(Defi).

As its name suggest decentralized finance, everything will be decentrilized from buying and selling shares to a individual payout. Here we will we predicting whether price of bitcoin will reach $20000 on jan 2021 or not.

Flow of the Project

  1. Creating a market for buying and selling the shares.
  2. collateral
  3. selling and buying of shares
  4. Resolving Market
  5. Result and Payout

1.Creating a market for buying and selling the shares.

In  prediction market, trading is split into shares.Each shares payout some amount, if the answer resolve to yes and if no it will loss its money.

2.collateral

To start market, a market creator must post collateral fort the payout, if the market resolved to yes, the collateral is paid out to the owner of 
the shares

3.selling and buying of shares

 You have to pay some fee in order to buy and sell the shares

4. Resolving the market

Oracle are used to get information from off-chain to on-chain , it provide the information whether our prediction was correct or not.

5. Result and Payout

When the result is out , collateral is paid out to owner of the shares.
